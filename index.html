Fantasy Hollywood

The Ladies' Version of Fantasy Football














Is your man always on fantasy football?

Does your boyfriend spend all his time playing fantasy football? Would you like to do something equally as time-consuming so that you both never see each other? Fantasy Hollywood is the game for you!

Start a league!

Just get 7 other lady friends and sign up for a league today! Each person takes turns adding celebrities to their team.

Play the slots

Each week, you place your celebrities in very specific slots based on what stupid, boneheaded mistake you think they will do. "Get engaged", "Receive a D.U.I.", and "Get a tattoo" are some of the more than 12 different slots.

Get the most points!

Each slot is worth a certain amount of points, ranging from 5-20. Each time a celebrity makes the mistake you predicted they would do, you get points.

Play—and beat—your friends!

Head-to-head match ups are played each week against the ladies in your league. And the best part? Since celebrities will never sober up and make good decisions, the league goes on all year round!
